  • Basic Training- 4 Hrs, Advanced Training- 8 Hrs
  • Training of Trainers (ToT)
    FSSAI recommends that all licensed food businesses must have at least one trained and certified Food Safety Supervisor under FoSTaC for every 25 food handlers in each premise.

Certifications

ISO 9001 (Quality Management System-QMS) Certification

  • Benefits of Certification
  • Increased operational efficiency
  • Consistency of process
  • Greater employee awareness about quality
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction
  • Reduced customer complaints

ISO 22000 (Food Safety Management System-FSMS)

Benefits of certification

  • Demonstrate an organisation's commitment to food safety
  • Ensure control at all stages of the food supply chain
  • Improve the Internal Processes to Provide safe food
  • Increases trust by the public & industry
  • Minimizes the experience of health risk foods

ISO 14001 (Environmental Management System-EMS)

Benefits of certification

  • Improve resource efficiency
  • Reduce waste & drive down costs
  • Less environmental impact
  • new business opportunities
  • meet legal obligations

ISO 45001 (Occupational Health and Safety-OH&S)

Benefits of certification

  • Create the best possible working conditions across organization
  • Identify health hazards and put in place controls to manage them
  • Reduce workplace accidents & illness
  • Engage and motivate staff with better safer working conditions

Workplace Assessment for Safety and Hygiene (WASH)

A scheme by quality council of India to help any organisation assess its preparedness to restart and run operations safely against covid-19. In order to ensure the business organisations, institutions etc. Focus on health, safety and hygiene condition of employees, customers and public will be of great importance.

USFDA Certification

The USFDA regulations are intended to assure that the manufacturer can manufacture, process, package, and hold a product to assure that it meets the requirements of the act as to safety, identity, strength, quality, and purity.

FamiQs Certification

FamiQs, Which is the Worldwide Quality and Feed Safety Management System for the Sector of Specialty Feed Ingredients. The FAMI-QS Certification Scheme Addresses Safety, Quality and Address the Challenging Topics of Integrity (Fraud), Defence and Regulatory Compliance

Trademark Registration

Trademark Registration Facilitates Brand Recognition for your Goods and Services. The Registration of a Trademark has Several Advantages because it Protects the Name of the Company and the Legal Right of the Owner of the Company

Halal Certification

Halal is an internationally recognized certificate and it is necessary to get trade permission in many countries. • Halal certification states that the products are acceptable for the followers of Islam and no haram product or procedure is used while its manufacturing or handling
